Dollar Liquidity Theory

Framework Implementation · v3.1

Overview

The Dollar Liquidity Theory is a macro-financial framework for assessing US dollar liquidity conditions across five levels — from abundant宽松 to crisis危机. It synthesizes Federal Reserve balance sheet data, money market spreads, and foreign exchange dynamics into a composite rating system used for weekly reporting and portfolio risk management.

Level Rating System

Level Signal T10Y2Y Threshold DXY Threshold Description
L1 宽松 Abundant < −50bp — Very loose financial conditions
L2 正常偏松 Normal-Easy −50bp to 0bp — Loose conditions
L3 正常偏紧 Normal-Tight 0bp to +50bp — Tightening, watch for transitions
L4 紧张 Stressed +50bp to +150bp > P75 Elevated stress, risk-off environment
L5 危机 Crisis > +150bp > P75 Severe dollar funding crisis

Primary Indicators

SOFR − DFF Spread

Federal Reserve repo rate vs. federal funds rate spread. Captures short-term bank funding stress. Rule: Level determined by spread magnitude.

TED Spread

3M Eurodollar rate minus 3M Treasury bill rate. Classic interbank stress indicator. Rule: < 30bp = L1, 30–80bp = L2/L3, 80–150bp = L4, > 150bp = L5.

Net Liquidity

WALCL − RRP − TGA — Fed balance sheet minus overnight reverse repos minus Treasury General Account. The core dollar availability measure.

L1: > $5.8T  |  L2: $5.2–5.8T  |  L3: $4.5–5.2T  |  L4: $3.8–4.5T  |  L5: < $3.8T

DXY (Dollar Index)

Federal Reserve trade-weighted USD broad index. Rule: > P75 (116.6) adds +1 level of stress. Elevated DXY amplifies global dollar scarcity.

Yield Curve Slope (T10Y2Y)

10-Year minus 2-Year Treasury yield spread. Inverted curve often precedes/reinforces liquidity stress.

RRP (Overnight Reverse Repurchase)

Supplementary indicator only. RRP = $0B is a wash — does not contribute to Net Liquidity or composite rating. Monitor for directional changes, not absolute zero.

Composite Rating Logic

The final rating is the worst level among all primary indicators:

  • SOFR-DFF and Net Liquidity are co-primary (equal weight)
  • DXY > P75 adds a "plus" modifier (+0.5 level) when评级 is L3–L4
  • RRP is excluded from composite — it's a supplementary signal only

Automated Dashboard

The Investment Indicators Dashboard is updated daily via GitHub Actions. It fetches live FRED data, generates AI commentary, and renders the latest readings for all primary indicators.

Data Sources

  • FRED (Federal Reserve Economic Data) — WALCL, RRP, TGA, M2, DXY, SOFR, DFF, Treasury yields
  • M2同比 — calculated as month-over-month change from M2SL
  • AI Commentary — generated via MiniMax M2.7 API
